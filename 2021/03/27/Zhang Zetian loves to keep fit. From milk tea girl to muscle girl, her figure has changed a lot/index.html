If you want to have ant waist and character muscle lines like sister milk tea, you can’t do without fitness, and fitness is also the healthiest and can prolong life.

The tension rope is very convenient to carry, and the movement is very simple. It can be exercised at home and in the office.

#Action 2: thruster sitting posture, two head bending

Exhale two hair force small arms up, inhale slowly restore. Each movement is completed between a breath and a breath, and then every time we exercise, we must fix our yoga ball, pay attention to safety, each group completes 12-15, repeat 4-6 groups.
