Action 1: belly rolling with pilates tension rope

Preparation tools: a yoga mat, a pilates tension rope

This movement is mainly to train abdominal muscles and shape abdominal lines, which is very suitable for small partners with meat on their stomachs. 15-20 in each group, repeat 4-6 groups.
**Action 2: lifting shoulder with pilates pull rope

Preparation tools: a yoga mat, a pilates pedal tension rope
Action tips: * stand on the yoga mat, fix the tension rope with both feet, hold the handle of the tension rope with both hands, stand straight, stop the waist and back, tighten the abdomen, put the hands naturally in the middle of the abdomen, exhale the deltoid muscle of the shoulder, raise the hands along the front direction of the body to the position where the big arm is parallel to the ground and the wrist is lower than the elbow, inhale slowly and restore, This movement mainly trains our deltoid muscle toe in to create shoulder lines.
