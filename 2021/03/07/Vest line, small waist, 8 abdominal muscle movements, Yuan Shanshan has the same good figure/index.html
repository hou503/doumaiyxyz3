Abdominal training program
Action 1: bend your knees and roll your belly

Action tips: lie flat on the ground, hands on both sides of the head neck, legs and head up, then waist force, legs and upper body inward movement contraction, and then extend at the same time. 10 in a group, do 4 groups.
Action 2: start at both ends

Action tips: lie flat on the ground, lift both legs and upper body, and straighten both legs and arms at the same time, then apply force at the waist and stretch both arms forward until they touch the ankle. In groups of 12, do 4.
Action 3: kneel and lift your legs

Action tips: first of all, kneel on the ground with both legs, straighten your arms, and put your palms on the ground. Then one leg is raised, the waist is forced, the raised leg is forced to rise, at the same time, the head and upper body also follow the upward movement. 10 in a group, do 4 groups.
Action 4: supine hip Bridge

Action tips: first, make the body flat, lying on the ground, and then bend the knee, so that the leg and the ground vertical, and two feet on the ground; Then put your arms straight on both sides of your body and spread your palms; Finally, the waist force, so that the waist and buttocks at the same time up, and then down. Do 15 at a time, do 4 groups.
Action 5: squat with bare hands

Action tips: stand with both legs as wide as the shoulder, put your hands in front of your chest, exert yourself at the waist, bend your legs to move your body down, and then straighten your legs to move your body up. In groups of 15, do four.
Action 6: supine leg lift

Action tips: lie flat on the ground, two arms on both sides of the body, legs straight, and then the waist force, legs up extension, until the legs remain perpendicular to the ground. 10 in a group, do 4 groups.
Action 7: touch your foot on the opposite side

Action tips: first lie flat on the ground, then exert yourself at the waist, extend your left hand forward to touch your right foot, then extend your right hand forward to touch your left foot, and move your head and upper body forward at the same time. 10 in a group, do 4 groups.
