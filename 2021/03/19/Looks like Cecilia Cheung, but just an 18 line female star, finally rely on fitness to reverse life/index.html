Looks like Cecilia Cheung, but just an 18 line female star, finally rely on fitness to reverse life

Posted on 2021-03-19

A person’s life can’t be a good time. Only with an optimistic and positive attitude can he get out of the predicament. When people are at a low ebb, they often abandon themselves and do not want to make progress. Today, Xiao Bian is going to introduce a female star whose career is at a low ebb - shamiya.

Many people will feel familiar at the first sight of her. She looks like Cecilia Cheung. But compared with Cecilia Cheung’s great success, her career has been suffering Waterloo. This makes her feel inferior, and her dream of singing seems to be more and more distant from her in reality.

But she still has the seed of singer dream in her heart. With her unremitting efforts, she finally signed the contract. The most terrible thing in the world is that we have tried our best, but in the end we have achieved nothing. Shamiya’s luck has been bad, no matter how much money the company spent on her packaging, but she is still not invited to any advertising.

After exhausting all her strength, shamiya has no courage to face the questioning eyes and voices of the outside world. She enters a state of eating at home without leaving home, and even suffers from depression. After a period of decadence, encouraged by her friends, she came into contact with fitness. Often in the company of friends to do all kinds of strength training, sweat hard every day, so that her body’s negative energy will disappear.

With the gradual obsession with fitness, her mentality is getting better and better. Fitness has completely cured her depression, and she will no longer question the value of her existence. After professional fitness training, she successfully obtained the fitness certificate and became a coach.

With the change of mentality and figure, she started her own fitness career. The pressure brought by her previous work has gone with the wind, and she will never cry and eat too much at night. In fact, fitness can alleviate depression, which is why shamiya opens a new door to her life.

Next, Xiaobian will share with you some of the physical training that shamiya often does. Insisting on doing a few groups of movements can improve the posture and make the body curve more sexy.
Action 1: lie on your side and raise your legs before and after

First of all, I’d like to introduce a set of thin leg movements. The first step is to lie on your side, one hand next to your ears and the other hand in front of you to fix your body. The second step is to put the left foot on the base, raise the right foot high and straighten the leg, and pay attention to the toe straightening. The third step is to swing the leg back and forth with the hip as the axis of the right foot, and feel the stretching feeling of the leg muscles. This action is 20 at a time. Do 3-4 groups according to your own situation every day.
Action 2: supine belly roll

The first step is to lie flat on the mat, straighten your legs, straighten your hands, and then put them above your head. The second part uses the strength of your abdomen to sit up slowly until you can touch your toes. It should be noted that the movement needs to slow down, so that we can feel the strength of the abdomen, which is also conducive to the protection of our lumbar spine. This action is 20 at a time. Do 3-4 groups according to your own situation every day.
Action 3: dynamic stimulation of abdomen

The first step is to lie flat on the mat, with both hands on both sides. The second step is to slowly raise the legs, bend them, and form a 100 degree angle with the thighs. When slowly raising the head and neck, you need to pay attention to let the back completely stick to the mat. The third step is to swing your arms up and down, inhale 5 times, exhale 5 times, and insist on sitting 100 times at a time.
Action 4: squat in a standing position

The first step is to stand on the mat, open your feet to shoulder width, then inhale, raise your arms to both sides of your head, and slowly squat down to exhale. Then inhale, imagine sitting like a chair, arms up, exhale and return to the starting position. Pay attention to this action, standard action, 10 at a time, 2 groups a day.
