How to shape 3D shoulder muscles Share 6 effective and practical deltoid training moves

Posted on 2021-03-31


Why is the shoulder so narrow? Many people feel that their shoulders are not wide enough, so they want to improve their shoulder muscles. But there are many ways to improve their shoulder muscles. I don’t know which one you are best at? Today, let’s talk about the five movements of shoulder training. Which one do you like best?

1. Sitting dumbbell press
If you can only choose one way to exercise your shoulders, then this movement is a good choice. Some people may think that barbells work better because the bar moves from the front of the body to the top of the head, which recruits deltoid forearms to complete the action.

On the contrary, the dumbbell press can apply tension to the plane above the shoulder instead of the inclined plane. This transfers part of the load into the triangle. Dumbbell recommendation needs more balance and coordination. That’s why it’s more effective.
2. Fixed instrument recommendation
Although the training effect of Smith machine is similar to that of barbell in theory, there are many devices that can increase the load and have a separate grip to complete the strength of one hand and two hands. With shoulder movement, you can move in multiple planes on these instruments.

Hummer equipment is the most common equipment, many fitness enthusiasts found that these machines can be better than Smith’s machine, can stimulate deltoid muscle more comprehensively. You just need to keep the core of your body stable, because it doesn’t need to control stability, so you can focus on stimulating the deltoids.
3. Dumbbell side lift
Although compound exercise is a necessary condition to increase shoulder circumference, it is difficult for you to develop strong deltoid muscle if you don’t exercise the middle bundle of deltoid muscle. The side lift is aimed at the middle bundle of deltoid muscle, and the training effect is very good.

When doing the dumbbell side lift, it is recommended not to put the dumbbell at the lowest position, but to keep a certain angle with the body, so that the middle bundle of deltoid can continue to contract, and the effect is better. Under the standard action, it is recommended that each group do 8-12 times.
4. Rope side lift
Although the dumbbell is a very practical and effective instrument, it is not obvious in the arc line training, especially in the bending and side lift training. At the beginning of the training, the dumbbell only provides a small amount of resistance.

Instead, the rope solves the limit of free weight, which provides continuous tension throughout. Usually, the most common problem with rope side lift is that it’s too close to the gantry, which makes it difficult to find tension at the beginning of the action, and you can’t even get all the important resistance.
There are two ways to get sustained tension: one is to stand far away from the dragon’s gate, so when you start to move, you already feel a certain degree of tension; Another way is to hold the gantry with one hand and tilt your body slightly. These two methods can improve your training efficiency.
5. Dumbbell slant board bird
If you don’t practice the deltoid posterior bundle well, then your deltoid will not feel perfect, because the deltoid posterior bundle is also very important to us. It makes our deltoids look more solid and full.

The best way to get the deltoid back is the dumbbell ramp bird, which can easily make the movement more stable and standard, so you will find that when you do this movement, the dumbbell will be lighter than standing and prone. You do this in isolation. You don’t have to practice with heavy weight. We try to do it 12-15 times in each group, and the effect will be better.
6. Reverse flying bird of butterfly machine
There is no doubt that dumbbells are an excellent tool for training shoulders, especially for all types of side lifts. But for some small partners, it is difficult to master the correct method. The correct method can accurately obtain muscle contraction and stimulation, especially in the posterior bundle of deltoid muscle.

There are many handlebars in the butterfly machine. We can try different handlebars to see which one is more suitable for us. We can also try to practice with one hand, because our body can be stable and we can rely on cushions. When we do this action, our elbow joint can be kept unchanged as much as possible. If we move the elbow joint, we may rely on the arm to exert force.
