If you want to have a good figure, but there is no way to get started. Let’s get started with the fitness moves we prepared for you!
Action 1 elastic rope dumbbell hard pull

Action tips: * first prepare two dumbbells, then two stools and an elastic rope. The elastic rope was hung on two dumbbells, and then we all stood on the stool.
Standing on a stool with feet the same width as hips, the dumbbell is placed between the legs. Then we hold one end of the dumbbell with both hands. Then we keep our knees slightly bent, keep our back straight, tighten our core, and lean down to our back parallel to the ground. This is the gluteus maximus, and the hamstring. There will be a full stretch, breath, and then stand straight, each group to do 12, do four groups.
**Action 2: stretch squat with dumbbell

Action tips: * first prepare two stools, an elastic band and a pair of dumbbells. The two dumbbells are connected and tied together with elastic bands. One is on the ground, the other is grasped by hand, and both feet are the same width as hips. Stand on two stools. Grasp dumbbell with both hands, bend hip, bend knee and squat down. Squat until your thighs are parallel to the ground. Keep the core tight throughout the process, keep your back straight, and pay attention when squatting. Keep your body straight up and down. Don’t rock back and forth. Squat, inhale, squat, exhale, 15 times in each group, do four groups.
**Action three leg weight-bearing hip Bridge

**Action tips: * prepare two dumbbells and an elastic rope, and then lean your back on the dumbbell stool. One leg supports the ground, and elastic rope is tied between two dumbbells. A dumbbell on the hip. The other leg is suspended and slightly bent. At the same time, exhale. Stop for a second at the top, then slowly drop and inhale at the same time, 15 times in each group. Alternate on both sides, 15 times in each group, do four groups.
