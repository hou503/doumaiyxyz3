South Korea's 52 year old frozen beauty dentist is like a sister to her 20-year-old daughter. How did she do it

Posted on 2021-03-18

As we all know, there are many beautiful women in South Korea. Today we are going to introduce a dentist. She is 52 years old and her name is Li Xiuzhen. Previously, she and her 20-year-old daughter participated in South Korea’s SBS TV program “different dreams in the same bed.”. However, Li Xiuzhen said that since her daughter went to high school, women seldom communicate with her. She can only send her photos on the Internet to find her sense of existence.

However, what netizens care about is not the contradiction between mother and daughter, but Li Xiuzhen’s young appearance. Mother and daughter stand together and can’t tell who is the mother and who is the daughter. They are really beautiful women.


Li Xiuzhen keeps such a good figure entirely through her usual exercise. From the photos she shared, it’s easy to see that she loves sports very much. In the past, Li Xiuzhen also said in the program that she has been training three times a week for many years, and it is uninterrupted. Her strong perseverance is admirable.

Some time ago, Li Xiuzhen came to the column of fasting people. Her daily life is either in the operating room or in the treatment room. When she helps patients with treatment, she often needs to bend down, causing backache. This is one of the reasons why she attaches great importance to exercise. As long as she has a break, she will do some simple stretching exercises.

Besides sports and maintenance, Li Xiuzhen believes that the best beauty secret is to keep a happy mood. She started using social media because she wanted to interact with more people. As long as you are in a good mood, you can shine naturally from the inside out.

Although 52 year old Li Xiuzhen’s figure is enviable, she can’t help eating desserts and cakes occasionally. But she always brushes her teeth after eating sweets. Maybe it’s because of her career. She pays more attention to tooth protection. Li Xiuzhen is not only beautiful in appearance, but also beautiful in heart.

In fact, Li Xiuzhen is very active on the Internet. She often shares her photos on Facebook and other platforms, which makes many men attracted by her appearance and figure. With the progress of medical beauty technology in South Korea, some people suspect that Li Xiuzhen has lost her face. However, her figure is gained through hard training, and she goes to the gym three times a week. It’s really admirable to keep training continuously.
Next, I’d like to share some of Li Xiuzhen’s exercises at home. If you are interested, you can follow her.
The first movement, shoulder training, takes you to practice right angle shoulder

Step on the foot cover with both feet, grasp the handle of the thruster with both hands, stand up straight, and then drive the thruster with both hands to pull up. Exhale when pulling up, and inhale when falling back. Do 15 times in each group, repeat 4 groups, persist for 21 days, and your shoulders will look better and better.

The second movement, compound training, can train the abdomen and legs to create charming lines of lower limbs and abdomen

Prepare the yoga mat, then put your feet on the foot cover, grasp the thruster with both hands, keep your body balanced, then slim your legs, roll up your body at the same time, and exert your abdominal force, 15 times in each group, repeat 4 groups, this movement will be a little more difficult, but you can also complete it. Must insist on practicing, your abdomen and waist line will be more and more beautiful.
