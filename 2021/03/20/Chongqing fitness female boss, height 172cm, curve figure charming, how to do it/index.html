Today, Xiaobian will introduce a set of hip fitness movements, so that you also have a hot body.
Action one barbell hip push

Action tips: * adjust the weight of the barbell to suit your own weight, then prepare a dumbbell stool, put the shoulder blade on the edge of the dumbbell stool, and put the barbell on our hip joint. We need to use a cushion to support our hip joint, otherwise it will be more uncomfortable. Then put your hands on the barbell to keep the core tight and the barbell stable. Do a hip push and push your hips up. When reaching the top, stop for a while, then slowly drop, exhale when pushing up, inhale when slowly lowering down, 15 times for each group, repeat for four groups.
**Action 2 dumbbell stool hip Bridge

Action tips: * prepare a barbell piece, and then put your feet on the dumbbell stool. If your feet are high and your body is low, you can also put a yoga mat on the floor to make it more comfortable. Put the barbell on the hip joint. Then lift your hips up and exhale. Stop for a second at the top and contract at the top. Then slowly lower down and inhale at the same time. This action can exercise our buttocks very well. If you want to raise your buttocks, don’t miss it. Do this action 15 times in each group and repeat it in 4 groups.
**Action three elastic ring single leg hip Bridge

**Action tips: * elastic ring single leg hip bridge, this action is to strengthen the unilateral hip muscle, we put the elastic ring at the lower end of the thigh, that is, a little above the knee, keep the core tight, and then put both hands and shoulder blades on the edge of the dumbbell stool. One leg in the air, the other leg on the ground, and then do hip bridge. Just like above, exhale when pushing up and inhale when lowering down. Each group does 15 times on each side, a total of four groups.
