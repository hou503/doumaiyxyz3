Korean female coach Jiang Xianjing not only has a Xi Shi like face, but also a very hot figure

South Korea out of beauty, whether in the film and television entertainment industry, over the years has been countless times proved.
However, we all know that the so-called “beauties” seem to be pretty much the same. Even if they are not the same, as long as they are famous, they will become more and more beautiful and more similar.
Frankly speaking, it’s all due to the developed cosmetic technology in South Korea.
Even now, many people scoff at this kind of “artificial” beauty.

Whenever I see such a beautiful woman, I think I appreciate it very much. I still need to bring a sentence “plastic surgery.”.

As a pilates coach, she not only has a beautiful face, but also has a perfect figure with a bee waist and buttocks, so she has gained a large number of fans on the Internet.

Really speaking, from facial features to figure, she may be regarded as a thorough “artificial beauty”.
But is “artificial” really bad?
It is the pursuit of more and more modern young people to have a beautiful figure, regardless of appearance.
Gyms are springing up.
In everyone’s wallet lie one or even several fitness cards.
From running, rope skipping, swimming to equipment, from reducing weight and fat to plasticizing muscle.
It is not a process of shaping one’s own “artificial” body?

For example, the Korean beauty coach, looking at her sharp curve without a trace of fat bee waist, full of traces of exercise, clearly slender and strong sense of vest line.
It can be imagined that in the process of shaping herself, she has paid a lot of sweat and efforts.
If such a coach guides you to keep fit, will you agree?

People used to say “eat by your face.”.
Because looks and body seems to be born, everyone’s starting point is different.
As if fate itself is not fair enough.
But in today’s era, there are more and more acquired means to make up for or even surpass the congenital gap.
The development of science and technology and society as a whole has brought us many ways to make ourselves more beautiful and better.
Why not cherish it?
In an interview with the beauty coach, she said that she likes the feeling of sports and sweating, and also likes her current state.
I think it’s suspected of self pity to be a pure natural beauty who says she likes herself. But it’s true and sincere for such a girl who is full of her own “hard work” to say it.

If we also want to build ourselves and strive to become “waist elite”, we might as well practice these movements more often
1. Plate support

Bend your elbows and support them on the ground. Your shoulders and elbows are perpendicular to the ground. Step on the ground with your feet. Keep your body off the ground. Straighten your trunk. Keep your head, shoulders, hips and ankles in the same plane. Tighten your abdominal muscles and keep breathing evenly.
2. Kneeling and tumbling

Bend your knees together, lift your feet slightly off the ground, lie flat on the floor mat or bed, put your hands on your ears, then use your abdomen to make your thighs work, and slowly lift them up until they are close to your abdomen.
3. Cycling in the air

Lying flat on the ground, hands on both sides of the body, back can be close to the ground, using the abdomen to start power, bending legs, make a bicycle position.

More healthy, more beautiful, more temperament, more popular.
It’s not a crime to cater to aesthetics, and it’s not even a crime to improve yourself.
Jiang Xianjing, who likes to sweat, is beautiful beyond national boundaries.
