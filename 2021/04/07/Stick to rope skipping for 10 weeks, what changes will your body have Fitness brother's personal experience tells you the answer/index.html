Stick to rope skipping for 10 weeks, what changes will your body have Fitness brother's personal experience tells you the answer

Posted on 2021-04-07


Many fitness people always think that rope skipping consumes more calories than running, and even think that rope skipping is ten minutes worth half an hour of running.

In fact, this understanding is wrong. It’s not that skipping rope consumes more calories than running. It has a lot to do with the speed, strength and intensity of training. If you jump less than 100 times in 10 minutes and run a few kilometers in half an hour, it’s obvious that rope skipping consumes less energy than running. But from the common situation, rope skipping is a good way to reduce fat, and rope skipping is easier than running.

I found a foreign fitness brother on the Internet. His name is Sam. in ten weeks, he changed from 164 kg, 14.3% of body fat to 155.8 kg, 7.8% of body fat, and developed his chest and abdominal muscles. During this period, he only used rope skipping exercise and some walking training, eating three meals a day to keep normal, did not participate in any other fitness exercise. Do you think you can’t think about it? Just use such a simple way to train abdominal and chest muscles in a little more than two months.
Now let’s feel Sam’s sports journey together!
Between the beginning of training, Sam had 164 kg, and the body fat rate was 14.3%.

After a week of training, Sam changed from 164 Jin to 162.8 Jin, and the body fat rate also decreased to 13.5%.

After two weeks of training, Sam became 162.2 kg and the body fat rate was 12.9%.

After three weeks of training, Sam became 161.8 kg, and the body fat rate was 12.3%.

After 4 weeks of training, Sam became 161.4 kg, body fat rate was 11%.
We can see that this month’s training is effective. Sam’s weight and body fat change every week.

After 5 weeks of training, Sam became 159.8 kg, and the body fat rate was 10.6%.

After 6 weeks of training, Sam became 159.8 kg and the body fat rate was 10%.

After 7 weeks of training, Sam became 159.2 kg, body fat rate was 9.8%.

After 8 weeks of training, Sam became 157.4 kg and the body fat rate was 9.2%.

After 9 weeks of training, Sam became 156.2 kg, body fat rate was 9%.

After 10 weeks of training, Sam became 155.8 kg and the body fat rate was 7.8%. After ten weeks of training, Sam’s weight, body fat and abdominal muscles have changed significantly.

Is still thinking about why rope skipping can achieve such a significant fat reduction effect? Let Xiaobian tell you today.
In fact, rope skipping consumes a lot of calories. For a person of 110 kg, if he keeps skipping rope for 10 minutes every day, he can consume 90 kilocalories, which is far more than that of playing ball or running.
Moreover, rope skipping also has many benefits for our body, such as helping us coordinate hands and feet, enhancing our cardiopulmonary function, and achieving the effect of fat burning and body shaping.

#Why can skipping rope reduce fat?
Skipping rope can help us burn fat. Of course, rope skipping can’t just hold the rope and jump with both feet in place. It needs some strength and methods. First of all, we should achieve the intermittent training mode, that is, the mode of speed change. Only in this way can we speed up the heart rate and consume energy.

At this point, many people can guess that the intermittent training of rope skipping is similar to HIIT, but the intensity is not as strong as HIIT.

Rope skipping has always been loved by fitness people, just like many stars, they often use this way of fitness, such as fruit sister Katie Pei, who also brings this sport to the stage, so that everyone can understand the benefits of rope skipping.

As soon as you see Hollywood movies, are you immediately attracted by the figure of the actors? Are you envious of their figure? In fact, rope skipping helps them.

Let Xiaobian tell you the basic steps of rope skipping training.
Step 1: jump with both feet for about one minute
Step 2: then squat in the lunge, 20 times in each group
Step 3: run and jump for 1 minute
Step 4: do push ups 10 groups
Step 5: jump with both legs for one minute
Step 6: support the plate for 30 seconds
Step 7: run and jump for another minute
Do 2-3 groups in this way every day, keep practicing for a month, and your figure will change obviously.
