Today, Xiao Bian will show you how Carly Claus works out everyday? What is the most common training action?
Action 1: pull the Kettlebell with elastic belt

This movement is mainly to train our buttocks, back and hamstrings. The first step is to fix the two ends of the elastic rope, and then clip the other end into the hip joint. When standing, the elastic rope is placed in front of the pelvis. The second step is to step forward, open the feet to shoulder width, straighten the back of the third part, tighten the abdomen, and lift the dumbbell with both hands. It should be noted that during the whole process, keep your legs immovable, knees slightly bent forward, waist and back straight, when you reach the top with force, you will feel the tension of your hips, and then extend your hips upward at a constant speed. Let gluteus maximus fully feel the squeeze feeling, in order to achieve the best exercise effect. 15 each time, 3-4 groups per day according to personal situation.
Action 2: ox horn bag load backward arrow step squat

This movement is mainly to train our hips and legs. The first step is to stand well with both feet together. The second step is to put the horn bag on the neck. The second step is to extend the left leg to the rear and extend it backward. Note that in this process, the back should be kept straight, the abdomen should be tight, and the knee joint should not exceed the toes, and should not touch the ground, 15 times per group, 3-4 groups per day according to personal conditions.
Action 3: dumbbell hip Bridge

This movement is mainly to train our hips. The first step is to lie flat on the mat and extend the legs to shoulder width. The second step is to place the dumbbell at the hip joint. At this time, bend the knees and feet to the ground and hold the hands against the dumbbell. It should be noted that during exercise, first tighten the waist and abdomen, inhale and stretch the hip to lift the dumbbell; When you reach the top, your shoulders, hips and knees should be in a straight line, your hips should be tight, and you can feel the muscle contraction of your hips. Then exhale and slowly lower, making sure to squeeze your waist and abdomen and keep your hips from touching the ground. 15 each time, 3-4 groups per day according to personal situation.
