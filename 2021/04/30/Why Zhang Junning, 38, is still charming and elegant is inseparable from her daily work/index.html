Why Zhang Junning, 38, is still charming and elegant is inseparable from her daily work

Posted on 2021-04-30


Now in the entertainment circle, there are many stars who insist on fitness, and everyone’s fitness consciousness is very strong. Many female stars have the habit of annual fitness, such as Mo Wenwei, Sun Li and Zheng Xiuwen. It is precisely because they adhere to fitness and diet control, so that they can still maintain a girlish figure in middle age.

Speaking of this, I have to mention a goddess of temperament. She is Zhang Junning from Taiwan. Her love for fitness can be found in many programs, and it is precisely because of her persistent love and self-discipline lifestyle that she can still maintain her 20-year-old figure at the age of nearly 40.

In mango station, there is a variety show after dinner, but as a guest, she can get up early in the morning and go for a morning run. And in the whole recording process of the program, she is also the first person to get up. In the photos on the Internet, we can also find that her daily life is a girl who likes to run in the morning and do yoga.

The most amazing thing is that she has run marathons many times, and she will do them whenever she has time. In the case of no filming and no work, Zhang Junning would spend her time in the gym. Even Peng Yuyan, the male god of fitness, said in an interview that it’s really rare for such a self disciplined person as Zhang Junning. From some of their interactive photos, we can also find that their friendship basically began with fitness.

Sometimes when I see Zhang Junning’s photos on the Internet, I always feel like I’m in the wrong place. At first glance, it looks like a picture of a fitness expert. In addition to morning running and yoga, she also enjoys meditation and some strength training. It is also because of her love that she has been keeping fit for more than ten years in a row. This kind of spirit and high executive power of refusing to compromise to her laziness makes her have a good figure that many girls dream of.

The reason why Zhang Junning, 38, has a 20-year-old body is that she not only controls her diet, but also keeps fit day after day.

Next, I’ll give you a group of abdominal training movements that Zhang Junning usually does. It’s no longer a distant dream to keep a girl’s sense. Let’s learn!
Action 1: supine with elbow touching knee alternately

Action tips: * first prepare a yoga mat, and then we lie on the yoga mat with our hands on our ears to keep the core tight. We should pay attention to our waist not to arch up, need to fully stick to the mat. Then touch the knee joint of the left leg with the elbow joint of the right hand. At this time, the other leg is straight. After this is done, it should be carried out on the other side, and then the two sides should be carried out alternately. This movement is mainly to exercise our internal and external oblique muscles, 30 times in each group, repeat 4 groups.
**Action 2: lie on your back and start at both ends

Action tips: * this action is mainly to exercise the upper and lower abdominal muscles. First, we prepare a yoga mat and then lie on it. At this time, we need a weight-bearing tablet. Barbell is a very good weight-bearing tool. Please remember that our legs have been suspended, not on the ground. Then hold the barbell with both hands, and roll up the upper part of the body. At this time, we need to take back our legs, then put the barbell piece on the insteps of my legs, and then stretch out my legs, and then the upper part of the body falls back on the cushion. We can exercise the whole abdominal muscles, 20 times in each group, four groups.
**Action 3: load rotation

**Action tips: * we need to prepare a medicine ball, and then sit on the yoga mat, with legs suspended and upper body suspended, and then the whole abdominal muscles should be tightened. Because this movement is more difficult, if your legs can not be suspended, you can put your legs on the ground. Hold the ball with both hands, and then swing left and right, driving our whole abdominal internal and external oblique muscles. Do not move too much, 40 times in each group, do four groups.
