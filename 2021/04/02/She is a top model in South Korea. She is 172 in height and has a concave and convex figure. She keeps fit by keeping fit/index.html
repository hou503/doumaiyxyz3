So for us, what should we do to have a good figure like sun Yunzhu? Next, let’s share two exercises that we can do at home!
Action 1, yoga ball sitting posture, two head bending

We’ve got Yoga balls, yoga mats, sportswear and a pedal rope. Then lay the yoga mat on the ground and put the yoga ball on our yoga mat.
Action tips: * tighten the core, straighten the waist and back, focus on the center of the yoga ball, slightly separate the feet, fix the yoga ball and keep the body stable. If there is no way to stabilize the yoga ball, you can put the yoga ball on the wall and make it stable with the help of the wall.
Then step on the base of the tension rope with both feet, palm up, and hold the handle of the tension rope. After getting ready for the posture, the serious illness clamps our body, exhales, the biceps force is small, and the arm rises slowly until the muscle reaches the peak contraction. Inhale and slowly return the arm down.
This movement is mainly to train our arm, biceps muscle, and make our forearm lines more aesthetic. One group completed 15 to 20 times and repeated 4-6 groups.
**Action 2: pull the rope to sit and raise the knee

**Action tips: * sit on the yoga mat, keep your back straight, tighten the core, and step on the base of the tension rope with both feet. Then grasp the grip of the tension rope with both hands, tighten the elastic rope, straighten and tighten the waist and abdomen, and then slowly lift our feet off the ground to maintain this posture.
Then exhale, legs and body slowly close to the middle, so that our abdominal muscles contract, inhale, body slightly back, straighten our legs.
This movement may be unstable due to lifting the foot off the ground, so we must tighten the abdomen before doing it to make the body stable. This action can train our abdominal muscles and make a good vest line. One group completed 15-20 and repeated 4-6 groups.
