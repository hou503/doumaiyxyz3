She started to work out at the age of 30 and became a new fitness girl at the age of 50. How did she do it

People who have been paying close attention to variety shows have more or less an impression on “Dear inn” starred by Liu Tao and Wang Ke. One of them is Liu Yelin, who is over 50 years old. She was swimming in winter, which made everyone feel very amazing and admired. In other variety shows, she has also performed weight-bearing squats, so she has appeared on all major information platforms.

In fact, her previous job was very common, that is, a librarian, living a two-point and one-line life all day. I’ve also been a nurse. Seeing her sexy body curve, no one can think of her past. She started fitness at the age of 30, and after 20 years of constant fitness, she succeeded.

When she was 50 years old, she was promoted to the status of “fitness girl”, and the efforts behind her were unimaginable. Fitness is the only thing that you can gain if you stick to it. In this long time, she has become an attractive woman with a devil’s body.

From her love of winter swimming, we can find that she is a more exciting person. In her spare time, she always takes part in all kinds of extreme challenges. For example, lay a mat in the white snow, and then start doing all kinds of yoga.

Now she has already become a fitness expert. When she goes shopping with her son outside, she is often misunderstood as a couple. It can be seen that fitness brings not only a good figure, but also a young face.

Next, Xiao Bian will share with you Liu Yelin’s usual strength training. If you want to have a devil’s figure and a young face, come and learn it!
Action 1: the gantry rope is extended

Action tips: * swing the pulley of the gantry to the bottom. Turn your body sideways to the pulley and fasten the rope to one of the outer feet. Hold the gantry with one hand, keep the body stable, tighten the core, straighten the back, and bend your knees.
Then move the center of gravity to the left leg, the outer leg will straighten the gantry rope, exhale outward, and tighten the hips. Inhale slowly, do 15 ~ 20 on one side, and then do it on the other side, four groups on each side.
**Second, stretch the hip with the elastic ring

Action tips: * * put the elastic loop that you can control on our knees. Support the body with knees and hands, push down on the ground, straighten the waist and back, tighten the core, and separate the hands from the shoulder width to maintain the stability of the body.
Then move the center of gravity to our left leg, lift the right leg slightly off the ground, exhale, force the right hip, and lift the right leg backward and upward as a body width movement. Inhale and return your right leg to the ground. A group of about 15 ~ 20 repeats, four groups.
**Action three, elastic ring single leg hip bridge

**Action tips: * put the elastic hoop on our knees. Lie on your back, bend your knees and keep your feet on the ground. Heel as close as possible to our hips, core tight, exhale, one leg straight up fixed, and then the other leg to support our body is hip up. Inhale and slowly drop your hips. Do 15-20 in one group, and then do the other side. Repeat four groups left and right.
In the process of doing the movement, we must pay attention to raise up, and keep the straight leg. Then the core should be tightened. When doing hip bridge up, the hip should keep the peak contraction, stay for 1 to 2 seconds, then inhale, the hip is slowly falling, close to the ground, do not touch the ground.
