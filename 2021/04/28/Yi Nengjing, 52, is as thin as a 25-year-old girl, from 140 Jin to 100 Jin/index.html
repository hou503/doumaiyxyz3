Next, let’s give you a group of Amway lower limb strength training movements, which can optimize the leg lines, reduce thigh fat, and raise the hips. Let’s practice with me.
Action 1: alternate squat with Kettlebell

Action tips: * prepare a suitable fox. Put it in front of us, feet apart, slightly wider than shoulders, waist straight, core tight. Then inhale, bend your knees, crouch back and down to the thigh parallel to the ground, lift Hu Ming’s body with both hands and stand up straight.
Then exhale and do another squat, with my thighs close to the ground and the weight on our hip muscles. In the process of doing, you can hold the Kettlebell alternately with both hands, or you can hold the Kettlebell with both hands at the same time. A group of 15-20. Repeat four groups.
**Action 2: stretch ring supine single leg hip bridge

Action tips: * prepare a yoga mat and an elastic ring. Lie on the yoga mat, put the elastic hoop over the knees of our two legs, bend our knees, step our feet on the ground firmly, with the hind tendons close to our hips.
Then straighten one leg, hold still, exhale, push the hips, tighten the core, and lift our hips up until we tighten our synchronization.
Inhale slowly and fall close to the mat. This movement can not only train our hip muscles, but also train our core stability. A group of about 15 ~ 20, each of four groups.
**Action 3: push up the side leg with elastic ring

**Action tips: * prepare a yoga mat and a stretch rope. Take talisai and kneel down on the yoga mat at the knees of our legs, support the ground with both hands, from a prone position.
Keep your back straight and your core tight. Then move your weight to one leg and lift the other leg to the side. Use the gravity of the elastic ring to tighten your hips.
Inhale, the knees slowly fall close to the mat, and then exhale, the lid is raised, and our knees tighten our hips. A group of about 15 ~ 20, each of four groups.
