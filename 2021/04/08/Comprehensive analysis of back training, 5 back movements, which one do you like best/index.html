Comprehensive analysis of back training, 5 back movements, which one do you like best

Posted on 2021-04-08


The back is the part that we must practice in the process of fitness. If we practice the back well, we can add points to our figure. The inverted triangle figure is always so charming, but it’s not so easy to practice the back well.

Many people don’t know how to match when they practice back. In fact, back training needs stimulation from multiple angles, because there are too many muscles in the back. When you practice back, which action do you feel most?

Which of the following five back movements do you like best?
1. Barbell hard pull
Technically speaking, it can not only exercise back muscles, it can exercise the whole back watch chain of our body, but it is absolutely the best choice for the whole back exercise.

The skill of hard pull is very important, but once you master the standard action, you can pull a lot of weight, which can drive more muscles, release muscle growth hormone, and help you increase muscle strength.
2. Wide grip pull up
It’s a great way to exercise your back, and pulling up is the best. Wide grip pull-up is very suitable for latissimus dorsi training. Although the narrow grip can give you more range of motion, the wide grip is more suitable for joint position, and the upward pull of the wide grip can exercise the back muscles more deeply.

Wide range pull-up is a big challenge for Xiaobai, because it requires strong back strength. For better back muscle enhancement, each group should do 8-12 times.
3. T-bar rowing
Choose T-bar rowing, because this action can pull more weight and stimulate the deep rhomboid muscle, but if the amount of training is too large, it may use the lower back to borrow strength.

The posture of this movement is not squat. You need to keep the knee at a locked angle, and then through the position and width of the hand, wide grip can concentrate more strength on latissimus dorsi, while the opposite grip can concentrate more strength on the middle of the back (rhomboid, teres minor and trapezius).
4. Rowing with wide grip
Sitting rowing is mainly focused on the back in training. Many people usually use V-shaped grip to practice. If you change to the straight bar and wide grip, the effect may be different. Because it changes the strength center of your latissimus dorsi.

Wide grip rowing imitates some back training instruments, and the way of holding or training times have changed. Don’t train with both. It can also be done in the way of general grip strength. The elbow joint is close to both sides of the body, and the strength will be more concentrated in the middle of the back.
5. Single arm dumbbell rowing
It’s a good one-sided training move, each side is trained independently, which ensures you can pull more weight. When you are training on one side, your range of motion will be larger. If your weaker side is exhausted first, you will not be limited. In addition, this movement can also strengthen the weaker side, and practice the two hands separately.

You can also put one hand on the bench to better support your body. A slight rotation of the trunk allows greater activity of the “core” muscles.
Of course, today is not a simple fight who is the champion, you need to know, from the difficulty of movement, the number of stimulated muscles, the uniqueness of training, to re organize back training, these five back training movements can let you know more about the shortcomings of your training system, so as to improve your training movements and movement arrangement.
In other words, if you want to train the perfect back muscles, you need to stimulate them from different angles, because there are many muscles in the back. Don’t train one or two movements, the effect will be very poor. Well, that’s all for today’s content. I like one click triple connection.
