Zhong Nanshan, 84, is still tall and strong. Fitness can strengthen the body and fight against aging

Posted on 2021-04-25

At the end of January 2020, the epidemic began to ravage. Too many people have homes and can’t go back. Some people are trapped on barren mountain roads and can only rely on bread to satisfy their hunger. The novel coronavirus pneumonia is much more terrible than we think, but many medical staff are actively involved in the epidemic prevention team. Now the shadow of the epidemic has gradually left us. When Xiaobian watched the anti epidemic commendation Conference on TV, he had mixed feelings.

In particular, “Zhong Nanshan” is our spiritual pillar. He is still struggling in the front line when the epidemic is spreading very fast. He is 84 years old this year. Despite the terror of the epidemic, he appeared when the people of the country needed help most.

When he received the medal of honor at the conference, his energetic mental state was clearly displayed. Even when he came on stage, he was like a young man, walking very fast.

He doesn’t look like an old man in his 80s, no matter from his behavior or body. He didn’t have the slightest bit of fat on his body. He looked like a budding young man in a stiff suit. The main reason why he can keep a good figure is because of his persistence. He has been keeping fit in private.

He once said in an interview that he had all kinds of fitness tools at home, such as treadmill, dumbbell and so on. And as long as he has time, he will not indulge himself, even when he is busy with work, he will take time to exercise.

This spirit of never giving up is exactly what we lack, but this is the secret of Zhong Nanshan’s ability to keep fit. Keeping fit is not a matter of one day, but a matter of gradual progress. As long as the patience to adhere to a top hour every day to lose weight, then you can delay.

Action 1: barbell neck back press

Action Essentials: * * this action is mainly the deltoid muscle of martial arts training. Prepare a barbell, sit on a stool, keep your back straight, tighten the core, hold the barbell with both hands and lift it over your head, exhale when pushing, and inhale when falling. Drop from the back of the head, 12 times in each group, 4 groups.
**Action 2: standing dumbbell side lift

Action tips: * prepare a pair of dumbbells suitable for your own strength, keep your feet as wide as your hips, keep your body stable, keep your core tight, and keep your eyes in front. Hold the dumbbell tightly with both hands and lift it from both sides of your body. Then we lift it to shoulder height and keep our elbow slightly flexed. Don’t straighten out completely. Otherwise, elbow joint will be damaged. Exhale when lifting and inhale when slowly lowering. Do 12 times in each group and do four groups.
**Action 3: alternate dumbbell standing position and flat lift

**Action tips: * prepare a pair of dumbbells suitable for your own weight, open your feet, stand on the ground, keep your body stable, tighten your core, face the front with both eyes, when exhaling, lift the dumbbell with one hand to the same height as your shoulder, and then slowly fall, 12 times on each side of each group, 4 groups.
