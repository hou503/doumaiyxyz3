Now there are a lot of fast-food movies and TV series, there are also many fast-food idols, which occupy our vision and senses. But those old classic films and powerful actors will still occupy a place in our hearts, which is the influence of classics.

That year, the idol drama of “Princess and little sister” can also be called a classic drama, in which the “Fahrenheit” men’s group was all the rage at that time. Among them, Wu Zun and Chen Yiru are still active on the screen, while Chen Yiru is now 40 years old, but he has a very good figure.

Many stars always keep their young spirit just like eating immortal elixir. Chen Yiru is such a male star. But he didn’t eat elixir. He kept his figure by exercising. No matter how busy his work is, he will go to the gym at least three times a week. Now I can see that his figure is not greasy like that of a middle-aged man in his forties. Instead, he is very young.

In fact, there are reasons for this. The biggest difference between ordinary people and stars is self-discipline. Many people even give up on the management of the body, daily talk, but in fact will not pay action. If you want to have a good figure like Chen Yiru, you only need to do three things well.

First, diet
Keep the daily production of the body needs the heat supply on the line, do not eat too many calories, regular diet is very important.
The second point: the law of work and rest
Don’t stay up late. You’d better go to bed before 11 p.m. Staying up late will also make the body fat accumulation, but also affect people’s mental state and physical state.

The third point: keep moving
Exercise can not only reduce fat, but also delay aging. In addition, it can improve the metabolism of the body and make our body curve more beautiful.

Next, Xiao Bian will share with you three dumbbell shoulder muscle strengthening exercises. You can follow them to build strong shoulder muscles.
Action one, dumbbell sitting position

Action tips: * sit on the dumbbell stool, slightly retract the shoulder blades, tighten the core, hold the dumbbell in both hands, push the dumbbell up with the strength of the shoulder, exhale when pushing up, keep the elbow joint slightly bent, do not completely straighten, then slowly put it down, inhale at the same time, repeat this operation, 12 times in each group, do 4 groups.
**Move two, dumbbell side lift

Action tips: * open your feet naturally, keep the core stable, and lean forward slightly. Hold the dumbbell tightly with both hands. The dumbbell needs to choose the weight suitable for you. Don’t blindly weigh it. Take a breath. Lift the dumbbell from the side to the same height as the shoulder with both hands at the same time, stop for a second, then slowly return to the falling position, and inhale at the same time, Pay attention to keep your body stable and don’t shake. Do it 12 times in each group and do it in 4 groups.
**Action 3: dumbbell bends over the bird

**Action tips: * sit on the dumbbell stool, lean over, hold the dumbbell tightly with both hands, lift the dumbbell from under the legs to the same height as the shoulder, and rely on the back of the deltoid muscle to exert force, 12 times in each group, 4 groups.
