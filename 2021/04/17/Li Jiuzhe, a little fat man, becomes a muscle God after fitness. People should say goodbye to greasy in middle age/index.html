**At the end of the article, I’d like to share with you some of the actions that Li Jiuzhe often trains. Let’s learn together
1. Barbell push

As shown in the figure, lie on your back on the flat stool, bend your knees to support the ground, fix your balance, keep your pelvis stable, hold the barbell with both hands respectively, the holding distance is 1.5 times of the shoulder width, put the barbell in the position in front of your chest, exhale and push the barbell upward, when you inhale, slowly restore.
2. Dumbbell side lift

As shown in the figure, hold the dumbbell with both hands and place it on both sides of the body. Keep your shoulders relaxed. When you inhale, keep the core tight. When you exhale, unfold your hands on both sides and make your arms parallel to your shoulders.
3. Squat

Separate legs, keep the same width as hip, keep the upper body straight, keep the core tight, inhale, slowly squat, keep the squat position, keep the big and small legs folded to 90 degrees, when exhaling, slowly get up and repeat this action.
