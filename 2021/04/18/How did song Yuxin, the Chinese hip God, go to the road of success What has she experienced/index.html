How did song Yuxin, the Chinese hip God, go to the road of success What has she experienced

Posted on 2021-04-18

The name of song Yuxin may sound strange. But in China’s fitness circle, she is a real celebrity. Fame comes from strength. This girl, born in 1994, won the title of “Chinese hip God” with her strong figure and proud double buttocks. It’s really cool for a fitness person to be famous for his body.

Song Yuxin’s life is not plain sailing. On the contrary, she has suffered more and experienced more than ordinary people. Two years old, just weaned, she was sent to a boarding kindergarten; When she was 9 years old, her parents divorced and she lost her whole family; Song Yuxin, 12 years old, unfortunately suffered from depression and attempted suicide many times because of his illness; Until the age of 17, this fearless girl went to a foreign country alone to study yoga in India.

No matter how hard life is, it has to change. Change might as well start from oneself, perhaps just from that time, song Yuxin found a new direction of life, began to gorgeous transformation.
No one is born a goddess. Beauty comes at a price. Song Yuxin, who used to be, was just the most humble one in the crowd, with ordinary appearance, flat figure, and even a “little fat girl”. But fitness changed that. Countless days and nights in the gym, sweat wet clothes and body, but not wet song Yuxin become better heart“ Fighting and movement are my life energy. She said. It is precisely because of such persistence and persistence that song Yuxin has a healthy and beautiful figure.

She can finally take a better picture. On the “men’s wear”, as if she is beautiful and self-confidence proof.

Today, song Yuxin has his own yoga studio and gym. Harvest a happy love and marriage. In the circle of fitness, there are countless fans, and every step is steady.

It seems that the stories about fitness talents are all inspirational. For example, song Yuxin, about her, seems to be a simple counter attack story about the ugly duckling turning into a swan. But we all know that an ugly duckling is born to be a swan. Therefore, song Yuxin’s becoming “fitness goddess” must not be just accidental. She dares to explore, dare to challenge, and dare to say no to herself who is not good enough. She is strong enough, strong enough, to stick to the hard road of fitness for ten years. As a girl, with a good figure in the public eye will naturally attract a lot of criticism.
But song Yuxin said, “you don’t have to feel sorry for your sexuality.” This sentence is not only for her, but also for all the girls. Who says sexy is not serious? It’s 2020!

If we also want to have the same “hip lifting” as song Yuxin, what training actions should we do?
Here are three tips for lifting your hips:
Action 1: kneeling position and side leg lifting

Four legged support, elastic hoop in the middle of the thigh, core tightening, one leg start force, lift the leg to the side, to the top of a second, and then slowly down, each group do 15 times, do 4 groups.
Action 2 step and side leg lift

Hold hands together, elastic loop in the middle of thighs, body left side step, and then the other leg up, and then the body right step, repeat action, 20 times each group, do 4 groups.
Action 3 elastic circle half squat hip abduction

Elastic loop in the legs, squatting posture, and then the legs outward, a little stay, to feel the power of the buttocks, 20 times in each group, repeat 4 groups.
The elastic circle plays a very important role in the process of fitness. It can be carried with you and does not take up space. Moreover, the training effect is also very good. Especially when it is tight, you need to control it with more force, which will double the training effect. It is very necessary to prepare an elastic circle.
