Liu Yelin, 53, has the temperament of 20-30 years old. How did she do it

Posted on 2021-04-26

Liu Yelin’s name first appeared in the public view, because of a hot post of “50 year old girl with a perfect figure of 18 years old”, which quickly spread all over the Internet. Now that she’s 53, she’s really famous, because no matter who sees her picture, they will ask: is this really a 53 year old woman? Beauty is common, 53 years old can still have 20 or 30 years old beauty, it has to be amazing.

With a good figure beyond people’s imagination, Liu Yelin has not only won “China’s top ten fitness figures”, “China’s most influential elegant women” and other awards, but also accepted numerous interviews and appeared in variety shows. In the program, in her 50s, she can still wear a bikini and run like a young girl. She is compact and does not show her old style.

Liu Yelin has been a standard beauty since she was young. But if you are more than half a hundred, you can still have a proud figure and elegant temperament, which does not come from God’s favor. About ten years ago, Liu started her own way of fitness, regardless of the incomprehension of people around her.

She likes all kinds of sports, such as diving, rock climbing and winter swimming. She especially loves to challenge the limit. She even completed the winter swimming challenge of Lake Baikal at minus 40 at the age of 50. Perhaps in her opinion, even the erosion of time and years can be resisted, what is the natural environment?

Liu Yelin’s popularity represents people’s admiration for “frozen age” and people’s real yearning for beauty. Her story tells only one of the most important things. It’s never too late to work out.

As an ordinary person, if we also want to have such a life, the simplest thing is to start from now on.
Here to share some common sports, usually work and leisure can do.
1. Push ups

Push up is the most effective and simple way to exercise chest muscles, hands slightly wider than the shoulder, the body straight.
It’s important to use your chest as much as possible.
2. Abdominal roll

Abdominal exercise is abdominal muscles, lying flat abdominal force, will roll up the upper back, leaving the ground, and the whole process of the lower back is close to the ground.
Abdominal roll should pay attention not to use the body’s inertia like doing sit ups. The purpose of abdominal roll is to exercise abdominal muscles. We must keep the movement stable and feel the burning of abdominal muscles.
3. Squat

The feet are the same width as the shoulders, with a shape of “eight” on the outside and about 30 on the outside °， Stretch your hands horizontally forward and squat until your thighs are parallel to the ground.
Squat in addition to legs, but also can exercise to many parts of the body, whether it is weight loss or shaping, long-term adherence will have a good effect.

Maybe not everyone can eventually become a “male god” or a “goddess” like Liu Yelin.
But the purpose and significance of fitness is not here.
Keep young in the body for a few more years, let healthy body eliminate the load of work and life, let oneself have more self-confidence in social life, is fitness can be achieved.
Liu Yelin’s not old is not a “miracle”, and the trick is to put it bluntly only a few words - love and persistence. Fall in love with fitness, fall in love with better you.
