He once weighed 230 kg. After being ridiculed, he decided to lose weight and became a muscular man in two years

Posted on 2021-04-13

At the beginning of fitness, people will feel tired, which is an essential process. However, when you see that your body is changing a little bit and becoming what you want, you will gradually like the feeling of sweating, because it is the transformation of your body and a better growth process of yourself.

There is such a boy abroad. He likes to stay at home at ordinary times, and likes food very much. He never exercises regularly, so he weighs as much as 230 Jin. When he looked at his fatter body, he became less and less confident. Even his friends would laugh at him from time to time. Therefore, he made up his mind to change himself through sports and let himself lose weight.

So he went to the gym and started his weight loss program. In the process of exercise, he experienced a lot of pain, because his weight base was relatively large, so some weight-bearing exercises he did more difficult than ordinary people. And he didn’t choose to give up, because he has a firm belief, he has always insisted, whether in the gym or at home, will train regularly.

Now he has become a standard man. In two years, he has become a fitness expert with an enviable figure. Through fitness, he becomes more sunny, energetic, and full of enthusiasm for life. Compared with him before, he is totally different. Fitness brings him not only a perfect figure, but also a positive and healthy life.

So if you are not satisfied with yourself now, Xiaobian thinks that the best way is to act and stick to it, so that you can meet yourself better.
Today, I’ll introduce 6 shoulder muscle strengthening exercises to make you have a perfect spherical shoulder.
Action 1: barbell neck back press

Action tips: first, open your legs and stand on the ground, keep the distance between your feet and shoulder width; Then hold the roots of both sides of the barbell with both hands, bend both arms, and make the bar of the barbell stick to the neck; The last two arms straight up, shoulder force, will barbell over the head. 15 times a group, 4 groups.
Action 2: barbell neck press

Action tips: stand on the ground with legs apart and shoulder width, hold the barbell root with both hands, and put the barbell on the lower jaw, then straighten both hands, shoulder force, and lift the barbell over the top of the head. Make 12 groups, make 4 groups.
Action 3: upright rowing with barbell

Action tips: first, stand on the ground with both legs, keep the body upright, then hold one third of the barbell with two hands separately, extend the arm downward, and then push the shoulder, bend the two arms, and lift them upward until the forearm, the big arm and the barbell are in a straight line. In groups of 13, do 4.
Action 4: standing dumbbell side lift

Action tips: the body is vertical to the ground, two arms naturally droop, two hands hold two dumbbells respectively, and then, two arms move along the arc on both sides. Until both arms are straight and parallel to the ground. 10 in a group, do 4 groups.
Action 5: alternate standing dumbbell with flat lift

Action tips: stand on the ground, hold a dumbbell in both hands, and keep the arms drooping naturally; Then extend the left arm forward until it is parallel to the ground. Then drop the left arm and lift the right arm. Repeat the same action. Make 10 groups, make 4 groups.
Action 6: bend over dumbbell bird

Action tips: stand on the ground with legs slightly bent, upper body bent forward, two hands holding a dumbbell, palms opposite, and then extend both arms to both sides at the same time, until the two arms and upper body are parallel to the ground. 10 in a group, do 4 groups.
