Next, I’d like to introduce Edson Brandao, who subverts the stereotype of Asians that they are over 50 years old. When I saw his picture, it was hard to believe that he was a 53 year old man. It seemed that he was a 30-year-old at most.

While his appearance and figure are amazing, his resume is also quite wonderful. In addition to being a famous writer, he is also a private teacher. This kind of gold-plated aura makes people curious about his daily life.

Looking at his photos on the website, you can almost find that his skin without pores is as tender as a baby. This unreasonable state of frozen age makes more and more girls become his fans. He began to run every day, and after running, he went to do all kinds of targeted strength training. In addition, he doesn’t even relax at home. He specially bought a lot of sports equipment to keep fit at home every day.

In order to restore his body to the compactness of his youth, he also specially consulted a nutritionist and planned his diet reasonably. As time went on, he became younger and more energetic. In fact, time is fair to anyone. The best way to delay aging is to exercise.

Next, I’d like to make up for the shoulder training movements that are often done by Edson Brandao of Amway. If you want to develop strong shoulders, please follow them.
Action 1: barbell press

Action tips: * * this action is mainly to exercise our deltoid muscles. First of all, the feet should be shoulder width apart, the body should be straight, the core should be tight, the eyes should look ahead, hold the barbell, put it on our clavicle, exhale and push it up. After pushing up, keep the elbow slightly bent. Inhale, put the barbell down, repeat 15 times in each group, do four groups.
**Action 2: upright rowing with barbell

Action tips: * * this action is mainly to train our deltoid muscles in front and middle. First, stand up straight, tighten the core, look forward with both eyes, hold the barbell, and hold the distance of the hand as wide as the shoulder.
Here’s a little trick, which is that we need to lean forward a little bit. Pull the barbell to the same height as the shoulder when exerting force. Then stop for a second. Then put down the barbell naturally and drop it to the original position. Do it 12 times in each group and repeat for four groups.
**Action 3: dumbbell bends over the bird

**Action essentials * *: this action is mainly to exercise our deltoid muscles. First of all, our feet are the same width as our hips, our knees are slightly bent, our body is bent, and our core is tight.
Hold the dumbbell well with both hands, lift the dumbbell up when exerting force, stop for a second when reaching the top, and then put it down naturally. Each group was done twelve times and four groups were repeated.
