1. Lateral extension

As shown in the figure, step on the ground with both legs, step forward with one leg, and the distance is one meter wide. At the same time, straighten the upper body, waist and back, bend down, and keep the legs straight.
The core should be tightened, the head should be close to the front side leg, and the back side of the thigh of one side leg should have enough stretch feeling. Keep it for 30 seconds and restore slowly.
2. Sitting side stretch

As shown in the figure, sit cross legged on the yoga mat, keep the upper body straight, one arm straight, lift up, lift over the top of the head, while flexing to one side, the other hand on the ground, maintain the balance of the body.
Support one hand to the other hand, open your waist and shoulder for 30 seconds.
3. Single leg support

As shown in the figure, the flat plate is supported on the yoga mat, the legs remain upright, one leg bends the knee, the knee joint contacts the ground, the body posture remains unchanged, and the pelvis remains neutral. Tighten the core and keep breathing evenly. Hold for about 1 minute, change the other leg.
