Move one, stand dumbbell alternate front flat

Action tips: * * this action is mainly to exercise our deltoid muscles.
Prepare a pair of dumbbells with proper weight, open both feet, tighten the core, keep your eyes in front, keep your body in a neutral position, and hold the dumbbells tightly with both hands. Raise the dumbbell on one side to shoulder height, then put it down.
Do the same thing with the other hand. Keep your mind moving in unison and feel the power of the muscles in the anterior deltoid. Exhale when lifting, inhale when falling slowly, 20 times in each group, repeat four groups.
**Action 2: standing dumbbell side lift

Action tips: * this action is mainly to exercise our deltoid middle bundle. First of all, you need to prepare a pair of dumbbells, and the weight should be within your own range. Then open your feet naturally, stand up straight and keep neutral. Tighten the core and look forward with both eyes.
Exhale and lift the dumbbell to shoulder height. Stop for a while and then slowly put it down. Inhale as you fall. The whole process to feel our deltoid in the bundle of force, each group do 15 times, repeat four groups.
**Action 3: dumbbell press in sitting position

**Action tips: * sitting dumbbell press pays more attention to exercise our deltoid anterior bundle. Of course, it also involves deltoid middle bundle, but it is only a small part. First of all, you need to prepare a pair of dumbbells with moderate weight, and then sit on the dumbbell stool. Keep the body in neutral position, sink the shoulder blades and tighten the core. Look ahead with both eyes.
Hold the dumbbell with both hands, palm forward, keep the forearm and forearm vertical. Exhale, lift the dumbbell to the top of the head, keep the arm slightly bent, and then inhale to slowly drop the dumbbell. Repeat 12 times, four groups.
