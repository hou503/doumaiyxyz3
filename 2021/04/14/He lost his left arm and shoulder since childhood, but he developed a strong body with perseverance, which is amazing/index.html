He lost his left arm and shoulder since childhood, but he developed a strong body with perseverance, which is amazing

Posted on 2021-04-14

Today, I’m talking about a special fitness enthusiast whose fitness videos have spread all over the world. He is not the strongest, but I admire him the most! Nine times out of ten, life is not satisfactory. When you complain about the unfairness of fate, someone is fighting against it. This is the master of today.

His name is Luke. From a very young age, he has been teased by fate. Arms, shoulders and most of the left lung. This is what he lacks more than ordinary people. Because they are different from normal people, they are often ridiculed. It makes him more and more sensitive. He even felt abandoned by the world.

Fortunately, he has the best mother in the world. Never give up on him, give him the confidence to live. It was because of his mother’s words that he was reborn in the fire.
“Although your body is different from other ordinary people, you can do whatever you want. Like ordinary people, you have to be brave. “
In addition to learning to smile and face life. He also learned that despite his physical defects, you have to be strong and brave.

When Luke first came into contact with the barbell, he fell in love with the fun of this power, and he was out of control from then on. Luke constantly trains his body, challenges his limits and proves himself with actions, which is no different from ordinary people.

Now, he has become a professional crossfit athlete. Although he has only one hand, his training intensity is no less than that of ordinary people, and even harder than others. He can push with one hand, lift weights with one hand, and play with one hand on the war rope.

Fitness is not an easy thing, not to mention Luke. It’s a natural defect. All his achievements are exchanged with sweat and hard work.

The following is a picture of Luke’s daily training. Let’s share it with you.






At the beginning of training is not easy, because of the lack of strength, must rely on the help of external forces, he was injured, also shed tears. But he still did not give up, smiling in the face of all the difficulties.

He often sees other people’s strange eyes in the gym. But he never cared and insisted on his exercise. Luke will also take part in various competitions. He didn’t want to show off his achievements. But there’s nothing he can’t do to prove himself.

Luke is now lively and cheerful, and makes his life full and meaningful. Use your own experience to make a documentary to encourage more disabled people. Although they have physical defects, they do not hinder the pursuit of the beauty of life.
You can jump to the limit; You can climb mountains and feel the beauty of mountains; You can challenge yourself by rowing. Around the world, no matter where he went, he left a sweet smile.


Luke didn’t give in and didn’t flinch. Challenge yourself, overcome destiny, live a perfect self!
