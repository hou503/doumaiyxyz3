Today, I’d like to introduce three hip and leg movements to you. If you want to have the same figure of anastasiya kvitk, please move together!
Action 1: Kettlebell squat

Action tips: * Kettlebell pull squat, mainly to exercise the lower limbs, can involve our hips and legs, want to shape the legs and hip friends can refer to.
First of all, we need to prepare a Kettlebell, then we need to stand up straight with feet hip width. Pull the Kettlebell from the ground with one hand, then hold it with both hands, do a squat, then put the Kettlebell down, then repeat with another hand, pull the Kettlebell up, do a squat again, repeat 20 times, do four groups.
**Action 2: Bulgarian split squat

Action tips: * Bulgarian split squat can exercise our leg muscles and hips. So girls who want to practice hip lifting and leg shaping can do it.
First of all, we need to prepare a pair of dumbbells, and then put one foot in a high position, which can be a bench, a step or a pedal. Then separate the feet, keep the core tight, then inhale when squatting, let us feel the stimulation of thighs and buttocks, and then squat up and exhale, repeat 15 times for each leg, do four groups.
**Action three, dumbbell hard pull back squat

**Action tips: * this action is mainly to exercise to the outside of our hips. First of all, we need to prepare a pair of dumbbells, two feet standing shoulder width. Straighten your back, bend your knees, do a hard pull, then stand up straight, then take a step backward with one leg, and then squat. Then repeat the action with the other leg, repeat each leg ten times, repeat 4 groups.
