Fitness three major, hard pull, push, squat, fitness new or old gun can not pass

Posted on 2021-04-10

“Three major events” – hard pull, bench push and squat, which are regarded as the representative training methods of pull, push and squat, will not be missed by either new fitness person or old fitness gun.
The three major events are also often regarded as the symbols of strength, and they are used as the main events in strength lifting training and muscle hypertrophy training. Therefore, these three movements and their variations will be involved in the training plan for muscle hypertrophy and strength.

Let’s take the simplest routine strength training plan as an example to analyze what a strength oriented training plan should be.

Monday: 5 × 5 (5 times in each group, 5 groups each time). The rest of the rope is pressed down 3 × 8-12 (3 times in each group, 8-12 groups each time). I’ll go boating 3 × 8~12
-The recumbent push will train the chest muscle, deltoid anterior bundle and triceps brachii.
-Rope down will train the latissimus dorsi, pectoralis and triceps brachii.
-Rowing involves the latissimus dorsi, constrictor scapulae, biceps brachii and posterior deltoid.
Tuesday: squat / hard pull 5 × 5, Romanian hard pull / front squat 4 × 6
-Squat and pull every other week.
-In the week of squatting, do Romanian hard pulls (RDLs); In the week of hard pull, do the front squat.
-On this day, you will basically practice the whole lower body (including the upper back and lower back to a certain extent): gluteus maximus, hamstring, quadriceps, and under the stimulation of squat, only the lower leg may not be practiced (but if you are a strength lifter, then don’t care about the lower leg).

Thursday: 5 × 3. Over recommendation 4 × 6. Weight bearing (if necessary) pull up 3 × 6
-The recumbent push trains the chest, deltoid and triceps.
-Over press will train deltoid and triceps.
-Weight bearing pull-up will train latissimus dorsi, posterior triceps and biceps brachii.
Friday: hard pull / squat 5 × 3, squat / hard pull 4 × 6
-The training content of this day is similar to that of Tuesday, but there are some changes in the sequence, number of groups and times of action. So if you squat on Tuesday, pull on Friday; If you pull hard on Tuesday, do a squat on Friday.
-Hard pull and Romanian hard pull will train to the waist.

The main purpose of this project is to increase the strength of the three major events and the whole body. All the “main action” are no more than 6 times, and auxiliary action is generally in the range of 8 ~ 12 times. About 3 / 4 of the training capacity is arranged in the main event, and 1 / 4 of the training capacity is arranged in the auxiliary action with more times.
Among them, there are 9 groups of squat and hip hinge movements (including Romanian hard pull and forward squat), which are less than the recommended 10 groups, but the main movements used to increase strength are basically close to the minimum recommended capacity. In addition, some actions that can better train the middle deltoid and triceps brachii are added to make up for the defect that recumbent push can only train the pectoral muscle, anterior deltoid and triceps brachii.
In addition, there are 9 groups of the capacity of downward pressure, rowing and pull-up, which also does not reach the recommended number of groups. However, you need to keep your shoulder blades tight and your lumbar spine extended when you do a hard pull. Secondly, in the hard pull and squat will also be the whole back together to practice. Finally, anyone who’s done a squat with a little weight knows that it depends on the muscles of the upper back.
Therefore, when writing the strength lifting plan, don’t deliberately let each muscle group be directly trained to at least 10 groups. As long as the capacity is almost within the recommended range, and there is a certain space for overlapping movements, then the training plan is no problem.
